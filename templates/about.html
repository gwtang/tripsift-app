tripsift was born out of my experiences planning a vacation to the Big Island of Hawaii. By reading through ~400 reviews, I selected the Mauni Lani Bay Hotel & Bungalows. While my experiences there did not disappoint, I had no desire to painstakingly read hundreds of reviews each time I planned a vacation. tripsift was thus brought to fruition during the Insight Data Science program. Please refer to the slides and my github for additional information.



FragFEATURE is a data-driven approach to predicting small molecule fragments that will bind a protein structure of interest. There are two parts to the project:
(1) the knowledge base contains all known protein-fragment interactions from the Protein Data Bank
(2) the method uses the knowledge base to make fragment predictions given an input structure.
Each fragment prediction yielded by the method is accompanied by all supporting pieces of information from the knowledge base to facilitate user interpretation of the results.

Publication: Knowledge-based Fragment Binding Prediction
